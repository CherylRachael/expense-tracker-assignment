Help & FAQ

Welcome to the Help & FAQ page. Here you can find answers to common questions and guidance on using the application.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

You can reset your password by going to the Profile/Settings page and clicking on 'Reset Password'.

How do I add an expense?

To add an expense, navigate to the 'Add Expense' page from the dashboard and fill in the required fields.

Can I change the currency settings?

Yes, you can change your currency preferences in the Profile/Settings page under 'Currency Preferences'.
